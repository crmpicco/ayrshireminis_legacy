INSTALLATION:

1. Upload directory "czar_green" to your phpBB under "{yourforumroot}/templates".

2. Login to your admin control panel. Under the "Styles" heading, click "add" then click "Install" next to the theme name.

3. Enjoy!

---------------------------------------

CUSTOMIZATION:

1. Open up "header.psd" in Photoshop and edit the text how you want it.

2. In the menu, choose "File > Save As". Overwrite the "header.jpg" file under the images folder.
